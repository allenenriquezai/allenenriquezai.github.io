3. No FB response time badge

Finding

Your Facebook page has no "Typically replies within X hours" badge. This means either you have not set it up or your response rate is below Facebook's threshold.

Why this matters

  • When a patient sends you a message on FB, the first thing they see is how fast you reply. No badge = they assume you are slow or that no one checks the page.
  • Clinics with a "Typically replies within an hour" badge get 40% more inquiries converted to bookings because patients feel confident someone will answer.
  • This costs nothing to fix — it just requires consistently answering FB messages within 1 hour during business hours.

What your competitors are doing

  • Any clinic with a green badge on FB shows patients they are responsive before the first message is even sent
  • This is a free trust signal that takes less than a week of consistent replies to earn

Fix

Respond to all FB messages within 1 hour during clinic hours for 7 days straight. FB awards the badge automatically. Or set up an AI auto-reply for after-hours so patients always get a response.

4. Stale Facebook content

Finding

Your Facebook page has no recent posts or has not been updated recently. To a new visitor, the clinic looks inactive or possibly closed.

Why this matters

  • Patients who find you on FB scroll through your page before deciding to message you. Old or no posts read as: the clinic does not care, is not busy, or is not even open.
  • Fresh content — even one post per week — signals that you are active, professional, and worth contacting.
  • This is also how you show up in Baguio Facebook group searches when people ask "any good dentist recommendations?"

What your competitors are doing

  • Active FB pages in the same niche post before/after photos, promotions, and patient tips weekly
  • Even simple posts ("Booking slots open this Saturday!") keep the page looking alive

Fix

Post once a week — a before/after photo, a slot availability update, or a simple dental tip. This can be automated so it never depends on you or your staff remembering.
