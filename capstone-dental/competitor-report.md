## Direct local competitor — ET3RNITY Dental Clinic

**Location:** Mabini St, Baguio City (same city, ~10 min from Easter School Rd)
**Web:** https://et3rnitydentalclinic.setmore.com/
**Verdict:** Functional but template-based. The strongest local threat to you right now.

What they have that you don't:
- Online booking 24/7 via Setmore (patients can book at 11pm or on Sundays)
- 26 Google reviews at 4.9★ (vs your 2 reviews)
- Service cards with duration and pricing visible
- Hero photo carousel
- Team page

What they DON'T have (your opening to leapfrog):
- Custom-designed site (theirs is a Setmore template — looks like every other Setmore site)
- Before/after gallery
- HMO accreditation page
- Blog or content
- Branded social presence
- Automated patient follow-up

**Net:** They have solved booking. You haven't. But neither of you has solved the full patient experience — the clinic that adds automated reminders, review requests, and a branded site wins Baguio.
